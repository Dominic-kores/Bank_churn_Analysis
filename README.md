# Bank_churn_Analysis
##  Project Overview
This project analyses **bank customer churn** using Power BI to identify key churn drivers and provide **actionable, data-driven retention strategies**.  
The main goal is to pinpoint significant factors influencing churn and recommend practical business strategies.  

**Tools Used:**  
- Power BI (Data Visualisation & Dashboarding)  
- Excel (Initial Exploration & Cleaning)

---

##  Business Problem
Customer churn has a direct impact on revenue and long-term growth.  
The bank experienced a **churn rate of ~20%** and needed to understand:
- Which customers are most likely to churn  
- What factors drive churn behaviour  
- Where retention efforts should be focused  

##  Key Insights
- **Engagement drives churn:** Inactive customers and those without credit cards churn the most.
- **Age matters:** Highest churn occurs among customers aged **41–60**.
- **Financial risk segments:** Low credit scores and balances **1k–10k** and **>200k** show elevated churn.


##  Recommendations
- Reactivate inactive customers with targeted campaigns
- Promote credit card adoption to improve retention
- Introduce premium services for high-balance customers
- Build a predictive churn model for early intervention

---

## Here are some business Impact
- Reduces revenue leakage
- Protects high-value customers
- Improves customer lifetime value (CLV)
